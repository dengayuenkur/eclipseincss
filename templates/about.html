Who We Are

Ethical Technology.
Local Expertise.

Eclipse Inc. is a South Sudanese tech company that provides excellent Information and cyber security services, Software Development, products, tech solutions and compliance. At Eclipse Inc., we offer Enterprise Solutions as well as Operational Solutions.

We have a team of dedicated professionals who have phenomenal domain expertise, by which we offer the best services possible while keeping your interests at heart.

Our Mission

Cybersecurity To deliver comprehensive, tailored cybersecurity solutions that proactively defend against evolving threats, while ensuring seamless user experience.
Software Development To deliver innovative and reliable software solutions that empower our clients to achieve their goals. We leverage our expertise and foster strong partnerships to create value, while staying at the forefront of technological advancements.
Security Consulting To deliver quality security assessment and consulting by proactive security analysis, instilling security awareness, recommending and developing robust security architectures, and working in partnership with security organizations to earn the trust and confidence of our customers.
Our Vision

"To be the leading force in empowering individuals, corporates and businesses in South Sudan through accessible and ethical technology. We are driven by the belief that technology should be a tool for good, and we strive to create innovative solutions that bridge the digital divide and unlock new possibilities for all."
